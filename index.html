About Me

I am Jindan Huang, a Computer Science Master's student at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Dr. Chien-Ming Huang and Dr. Russell H. Taylor. I am a member of Intuitive Computing Laboratory.

My research interests are Human-Computer Interation and Human-Robot Interaction, especially their applications in biomedical fields.

Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems at China Pharmaceutical University.
